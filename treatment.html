Therapies

Therapies, like physical, occupational, or speech therapy, are often recommended for chronic conditions or recovery from injury. They aim to improve function and quality of life.

Surgical Procedures

Surgery may be necessary for severe conditions. This can include minimally invasive surgeries or major procedures based on the patient's needs.

Rehabilitation

Rehabilitation focuses on helping patients recover abilities lost due to illness, injury, or surgery. It includes physical therapy, counseling, and other support.

Alternative Medicine

Alternative treatments, such as acupuncture and herbal medicine, are used alongside traditional treatments. Always consult a doctor before starting any alternative treatment.
